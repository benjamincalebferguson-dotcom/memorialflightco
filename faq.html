FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Straightforward answers to common questions about our service.

What is a memorial flight? +

A professionally managed aerial ash scattering service conducted over a selected coastal location. We handle every element of the process — from coordination to execution — with care and respect.

Where do you operate? +

We are currently limited to Melbourne and the Mornington Peninsula. As we grow, we will expand to additional coastal areas. Submit an enquiry and we'll confirm whether your location is covered.

Can family attend? +

This depends on the service type and location — we'll discuss options during the enquiry process and find an arrangement that works for your family.

Is this available now? +

We are operating in a limited launch phase. Availability is managed via enquiry and waiting list. Early enquiries are recommended to secure a place.

What happens after I enquire? +

We contact you directly to discuss suitability, timing, and next steps. There is no obligation at the enquiry stage — we're here to help you understand what's possible.

What if weather changes? +

Flights are scheduled with safety and conditions in mind. If needed, we reschedule at no additional cost. We'll always communicate clearly and in advance.
