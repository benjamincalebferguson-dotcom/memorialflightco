What is a memorial ceremony? +

A Memorial Flight Co ceremony is a private, aerial memorial experience carried out with careful planning, respectful handling, and optional filming for the family afterward.

Can family witness the ceremony live? +

Yes. A private ceremony link can be provided so invited family members may witness the memorial remotely in a private and dignified format.

Is the ceremony filmed? +

Yes. The ceremony can be thoughtfully captured and delivered afterward as a memorial film that families may keep privately or use at a gathering or service.

Do you work through funeral directors? +

Yes. Memorial Flight Co is designed to work alongside funeral directors as a referral partner, helping families access a premium memorial option without adding complexity to funeral operations.

Where do you currently operate? +

We currently focus on Melbourne, the Mornington Peninsula, and surrounding coastal regions. Availability can be confirmed during an enquiry.

What happens if weather conditions change? +

Weather and safety conditions are reviewed as part of every memorial arrangement. If conditions are not suitable, the ceremony is rescheduled with clear communication and care.

Are ceremony locations fixed or chosen case by case? +

Locations are considered case by case based on suitability, conditions, and the type of memorial arrangement being planned. We discuss this during the enquiry process.

Can the memorial film be shown during a gathering or service? +

Yes. The memorial film can be kept privately, shared with family, or incorporated into a gathering or funeral service if that feels meaningful and appropriate.

What memorial options are available? +

Available arrangements may include the memorial ceremony itself, memorial film delivery, and selected ceremonial additions such as floral elements or biodegradable urn presentation depending on suitability and planning.
