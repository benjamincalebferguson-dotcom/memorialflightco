For funeral directors

A memorial offering your families can experience, witness, and remember.

Memorial Flight Co is a premium referral partner for funeral directors seeking a dignified memorial option without increasing operational burden.

Why directors offer this

A premium family offering that expands the memorial experience.

Families increasingly want memorial experiences that feel personal, visual, and private. This allows your firm to offer a distinctive memorial ceremony option while maintaining the standards and tone your families expect.

Easy to integrate

We partner with funeral directors, we do not compete with them.

Memorial Flight Co sits alongside your existing service model. We handle the memorial fulfillment, private ceremony viewing, and memorial footage delivery while you remain the trusted family-facing advisor.

How the referral process works

You introduce the memorial option, connect the family to us, and we coordinate the ceremony requirements directly and respectfully.

What the family experiences

The family receives a calm memorial ceremony, the option of private live witnessing, and a memorial film delivered afterward.

What Memorial Flight Co handles

We manage memorial timing, flight operations, ceremony fulfillment, weather review, and memorial film delivery.

There is no need for your team to manage the ceremony logistics directly. We provide a straightforward referral model and clear communication.
