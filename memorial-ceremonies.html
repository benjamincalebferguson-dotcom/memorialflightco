Memorial ceremonies

A memorial ceremony that can be witnessed, remembered, and thoughtfully kept.

Aerial memorial ceremonies, optional live viewing, and memorial film delivery for families seeking something calm, visual, and deeply respectful.

What it is

A memorial ceremony, not simply a service transaction.

A Memorial Flight Co ceremony is designed as a dignified farewell experience. The ceremony is handled carefully and can be shared through a live link or a memorial film that remains with the family afterward.

Live viewing

When appropriate, a private ceremony link can be provided so invited family members may witness the memorial in real time from wherever they are.

Memorial film

The ceremony can be filmed and delivered afterward as a memorial record that families may keep or share within a gathering or service.

Optional flowers

A floral component can be arranged to create a softer visual tribute and a more complete ceremonial atmosphere.

Biodegradable urn release

For families seeking a more fully composed memorial presentation, biodegradable urn and floral arrangements may be discussed during planning.
