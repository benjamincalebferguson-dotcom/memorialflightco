More than a service

More than a service, a memorial experience.

Memorial Flight Co offers a memorial ceremony that can be witnessed live through a secure ceremony link, shared with family, or incorporated into a funeral service or family gathering.

Each ceremony is thoughtfully filmed and delivered afterward as a memorial film, giving families a lasting record of a farewell that feels calm, respectful, and beautifully carried out.

How it works

A calm process, guided in five thoughtful steps.

01
Offered through your funeral director

The memorial ceremony is introduced as a premium, coordinated offering for the family.

02
Ashes are arranged and timing is requested

We confirm the memorial details, preferred timing, and the setting most suitable for the ceremony.

03
A private ceremony link is provided

Family members can witness the memorial live from wherever they are, with a calm and respectful viewing experience.

04
The ceremony is carried out and filmed

The memorial is professionally conducted and thoughtfully captured in a calm, discreet way.

05
The family receives the memorial footage

The final memorial film can be kept, shared with family, or shown at a gathering or service.
