Privacy policy

We handle personal information with discretion and care.

This page explains how Memorial Flight Co handles enquiry details submitted through the website.

Information we collect

When you submit an enquiry, we may collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, and any details you choose to provide about your enquiry.

How we use it

We use this information to respond to your enquiry, discuss memorial ceremony suitability, arrange follow-up communication, and provide information about our services.

How form submissions are handled

Website enquiry forms are processed through a secure third-party form service provider so submissions can be delivered to us promptly.

Privacy and discretion

Memorial enquiries are treated with discretion. We do not use submitted information for unrelated marketing without consent.

Questions

If you have a privacy-related question, please contact us through the contact page and we will respond directly.
