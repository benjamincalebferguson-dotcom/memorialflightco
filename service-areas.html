Where we operate

Beautiful coastal settings across Victoria.

We arrange memorial ceremonies across Melbourne and the Mornington Peninsula — some of Victoria's most meaningful coastal landscapes.

Melbourne & Port Phillip

Families and funeral directors across greater Melbourne may enquire for ceremony availability and planning. Port Phillip Bay offers a range of meaningful coastal settings within reach of the city.

Mornington Peninsula

The Mornington Peninsula is a key focus area for ceremonies. Its protected bays, open coastal stretches, and clear turquoise waters — including Rye, Rosebud, and Sorrento — provide a calm and dignified setting.
