How it works

A clear process, designed to feel calm for families and simple for funeral directors.

Every stage is handled with care, from referral and timing through to private viewing and memorial film delivery.

The process

Five carefully managed steps.

01

Offered through your funeral director

The memorial ceremony is presented as a premium option that can sit naturally within the family’s existing funeral planning.

02

Ashes are arranged and memorial timing is requested

We confirm the ceremony timing, location suitability, and the practical details needed to prepare the memorial respectfully.

03

A private ceremony link is provided

Where appropriate, invited family members receive a private link so they can witness the memorial live from wherever they are.

04

The memorial ceremony is carried out and filmed

The ceremony is performed professionally, discreetly, and with the visual care needed for meaningful memorial footage.

05

The family receives the memorial footage

The memorial film is delivered afterward so it can be kept, shared with loved ones, or incorporated into a funeral service or family gathering.

Careful timing

Memorial timing is arranged to suit conditions, location, and the family’s broader service schedule.

Private experience

The ceremony is designed to feel private and dignified rather than public or procedural.

Clear communication

Families and funeral directors receive straightforward information about timing, logistics, and next steps.

Professional fulfillment

Flight operations, weather review, and memorial filming are handled with discretion and professionalism.

Ready to speak?

We can walk you through timing, viewing, and the memorial film with care and clarity.
